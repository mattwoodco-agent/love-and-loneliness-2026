# Love & Loneliness in 2026

A GitHub Pages site exploring contemporary themes of love and loneliness based on recent research and news from 2026.

## 📋 Overview

This site examines the current state of human connection in our digital age, drawing from recent studies, news reports, and research findings from 2026. It covers topics including:

- The concept of "social asymmetry" and its health implications
- Rise of AI relationships and digital companionship
- The "male loneliness epidemic" discourse
- Gen Z dating patterns and their workplace implications
- Latest research on the neurochemistry of love and loneliness

## 🔬 Key Research Highlighted

### Social Asymmetry Study (Cornell University, 2026)
- Published in JAMA Network Open
- Introduces concept of gap between objective social isolation and subjective loneliness
- Links this mismatch to increased mortality and disease risk
- Challenges traditional approaches to addressing loneliness

### AI Relationships Research (APA, 2026)
- Examines growing trend of human-AI emotional bonds
- Investigates impacts on social skills and intimacy
- Considers mental health implications of AI companionship

### Gen Z Dating Patterns (Fortune, 2026)
- Links reduced dating frequency to workplace performance
- Explores connection between relationship experience and professional social skills

## 🚀 Local Development

To run this site locally:

1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/mattwoodco-agent/love-and-loneliness-2026.git
   ```

2. Open `index.html` in your web browser or serve with a local web server:
   ```bash
   python -m http.server 8000
   ```

3. Navigate to `http://localhost:8000`
